Teamwork

We encourage our contractors to work together to solve the challenges they encounter.

Our contractors don't have set teams and rotate who they are paired with.

We continually improve our ability with debriefs and feedback after each contract.

Every member is encouraged to share their challenges and triumphs.

Specialization

Our contractors have the ability to expand their skill set and provide specialized support to their team.

Our training is flexible and provides a quick route to certifications for those joining with existing knowledge, or in-depth training for those learning a new skill.

Some of our specialized roles include:

Medic | Marksman | Autorifleman | EOD

Persistence

All gear is persistent. Everything you take into the field needs to be brought back, or it is lost. We work to recover our fallen teammate's gear to help them equip back up to join us at the next contract.

Customization

Use payments from contracts to purchase your gear. You are able to use anything from our growing shop. There are no requirements for uniforms, vests, helmets, or backpacks. You are free to decide your own kit.
